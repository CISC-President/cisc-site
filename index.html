Our Club

More than a season.

The Cambridge-Isanti Soccer Club is committed to providing the opportunity for children to play soccer in a positive environment, while engaging parents and the community to take an active role in developing our young people.

Our purpose is to promote, foster, and perpetuate the game of soccer at the youth level, with fun and enjoyable programs suitable for all ages at both the recreational and competitive level.

What we stand for

Every player, every family, every volunteer makes CISC what it is. We grow the game by growing people first.
